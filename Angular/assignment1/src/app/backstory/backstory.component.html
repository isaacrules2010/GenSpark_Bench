I have always lived in the dark, whether it be the mausoleum on my family's property or the hidden thoroughfares in whatever town or city i find myself in at the moment...

So... maybe that's why I wasn't fazed when I woke up the night after the heist and couldn't see or hear anything.

I had been hired, along with a cobbled together crew, to rob some eccentric's penthouse... Apparently we weren't looking for anything in particular, just kind of... sending a message... I can't even remember the asshole's name...

But I DO remember seeing that damned crystal.

It was sitting on the desk. Plain as day. Like it was an afterthought... and why wouldn't it be? It was big, or bright, or well-cut... Any appraiser worth even half his salt would've thrown it out.

I don't know why I took it. I don't know why I could hear it literally whispering my name, or why, when I touched it, the house suddenly got darker, as if the shadows were trying to smother us... I don't even remember how the rest of the heist went...

All I know is that when I woke up from the deepest sleep I can remember, I was surrounded by darkness. Silent, thick, palpable darkness that felt endless.

I sat there, in complete, maddening silence.

It felt like an eternity... I couldn't take it anymore. I wanted out. I screamed, but couldn't hear my voice. I was begging to be taken out. Somewhere. Anywhere. Anywhere but in the darkness, anywhere but here, I-

Then I heard it... laughing. Grating... yet melodic...

and suddenly I could see... It was still dark, I could tell, but somehow I could see perfectly.

I was in the middle of a black forest, the trees reaching high above, blocking the sky. I walked forward, into a clearing... i remember noticing that the sky had no stars. She was standing there. Waiting.

She offered me power beyond anything I could imagine, but said it would come with a price. Nothing is ever free anyway... and what she was offering was... irresistible to say the least.

Then I actually woke up in the bed I had rented at the local Inn. In the years since then... my life has changed. I'm well on my way to becoming the best gods-damned thief anyone has ever heard of... Built myself a name in the underground as the guy you call when someone says the word "impossible."

Sure, I'm bound to... I still haven't learned her name... I really should get on that. Only... I haven't spoken to her since that night... Now it's some new shadow demon every other week, giving me orders like they run the place. It's whatever, I do what they want and I get to keep my power... and the wealth and influence that come with it.

What could be better?
